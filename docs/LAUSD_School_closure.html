Los Angeles Unified Update on Fire and School Closures (1-08-25)

洛杉矶，加州（2025 年 1 月 8 日） – 洛杉矶联合学区（洛杉矶联合学区）中东部地区（曼彻斯特大道/费尔斯通大道以北、第 10 大道以东、710 号州际公路以西和 134 号高速公路以南）的学校由于空气质量恶化，今天将关闭。完整的学校名单将在稍后公布。随着伊顿火灾的迅速蔓延，情况在一夜之间急剧变化，我们谨慎地遵循科学建议关闭学校。今天所有体育活动、比赛和练习都取消。此前宣布的六所学校将继续关闭。其他地区应谨慎行事并做好自我保护。学区将对时间表、家庭和员工提供灵活性和宽限期，包括受影响最严重的学校可以调整为最小化课程安排。患有慢性疾病和/或呼吸系统疾病的人员不应去学校或工作。有关最新信息，请在 Twitter 上关注洛杉矶联合学区 @laschools 和 @lausdsup，在 Instagram 上关注 @laschools 和 @lausdsup，以及在 Facebook 上关注 @laschools 和 @AlbertoMCarvalho1。 ### 总部 - 洛杉矶市博德瑞大道 333 号，加州 90017 电话： (213) 241-1000

All Los Angeles Unified Schools and Offices Closed on Thursday, Jan. 9

**News Release** **CONTACT:** communications@lausd.net **FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE** **Jan. 8, 2025, as of 4:00 p.m.** --- **All Los Angeles Unified Schools and Offices Closed on Thursday, Jan. 9** **Joint Statement from Superintendent Alberto M. Carvalho, Board President Scott M. Schmerelson, and Board Vice-President Dr. Rocío Rivas** Los Angeles, CA (Jan. 8, 2025, as of 4:00 p.m.) – In light of the ongoing unprecedented crisis affecting the Los Angeles region, the health and safety of our students and employees remain our highest priority. As a result, all Los Angeles Unified schools and offices will be closed on Thursday, Jan. 9. The combination of wind, fire, and smoke has created dangerous and complex situations that pose unsafe conditions for our school communities. Essential personnel will be contacted by their supervisors regarding any potential work duties during this time. To support our students' learning during this closure, we are ensuring that they have continued digital access to academic resources. More information can be found at [lausd.org/learningcontinues](https://www.lausd.org/learningcontinues). The District will assess the situation over the next 24 hours and is preparing for the possibility of online learning on Friday, Jan. 10. A decision regarding operations for Friday will be announced by 4:00 p.m. on Thursday. **Available resources include the following:** For the most up-to-date information, please follow Los Angeles Unified on: - Twitter/X: @laschools, @lausdsup - Instagram: @laschools, @lausdsup - Facebook: @laschools, @AlbertoMCarvalho1 - Bluesky: @laschools.bsky.social, @lausdsup.bsky.social ### **Headquarters** 333 South Beaudry Avenue, Los Angeles, CA 90017 Phone: (213) 241-1000
