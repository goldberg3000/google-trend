SAG Awards 2025: See All the Nominees for the 31st Screen Actors Guild Awards Here | Vogue

One of the more important—and beloved—award ceremonies all season, the SAG Awards are set this year for Sunday, February 23, at the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles. There, Nobody Wants This star Kristen Bell will serve as the evening’s host, while Jane Fonda is set to recieve a richly deserved Lifetime Achievement Award. Like last year, the show will stream live globally on Netflix. Nominees in the film and television fields were announced via press release on Wednesday, January 8, after plans for an in-person event helmed by actors Joey King and Cooper Koch were scrapped due to the devastating Pacific Palisades fires.
